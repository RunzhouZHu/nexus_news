How are connections between news items created?

This is the core engine of your product — who decides that Event A caused Event B?

A

AI-Powered Automatically

The app ingests news feeds and uses AI to detect causal relationships, link related stories, and build the graph automatically. Users just browse. Fast to scale, but requires strong AI accuracy.

B

User-Built (Collaborative)

Users submit news items and draw connections themselves — like a Wikipedia for cause-and-effect. Community votes surface the best-reasoned chains. Rich, but slow to populate and prone to bias.

C

Editorial Curation

A team of editors or experts manually builds and verifies connections. High trust and quality, but doesn't scale easily and requires ongoing staffing.

D

AI + Human Hybrid

AI proposes connections automatically; humans (editors or the crowd) review and approve them. Best of both worlds — scalable and trustworthy — but more complex to build.
