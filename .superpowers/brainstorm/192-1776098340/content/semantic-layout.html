Semantic Node Layout

When a node is clicked, its connections arrange by relationship type. Does this match your vision?

A — Semantic layout always active

The whole graph uses this semantic arrangement at all times. Every selected node anchors to center with causes left, effects right, context surrounding. No force physics — layout is deterministic and logic-driven.

B — Semantic layout on click, force for background

Unselected nodes float freely with force physics. When you click a node, it animates to center and its neighbors rearrange semantically. Clicking background returns to force layout. More dynamic but more complex to implement.
